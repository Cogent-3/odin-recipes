The 2023 Bean Dip recipe sweeping the nation

This recipe will put aunt Kim and cousin Sheila to shame.

AND its quite simple

This dish is a fan favorite at superbowl events, bachelor parties, and birthdays. Everyone loves bean dip.

The ingredients are as follows:

  • Beans
  • Guac
  • Queso Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Nacho Chips

The steps to make it are quite easy too

  1. Gather ingredients and large enough bowl
  2. Add base of bean dip to bottom of bowl
  3. Add guac, queso, salsa, sour cream, and shredded cheese
  4. Start serving with nachos!
