The 2023 Hot Dog recipe sweeping the nation

This recipe will put cousin Steve and couch Jeremy to shame.

AND its quite simple

This dish is a fan favorite at softball games, street venders, and grill parties. Everyone loves dogs.

The ingredients are as follows:

  • hotdog
  • buns
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • relish
  • Shredded Cheese
  • chili

The steps to make it are quite easy too

  1. Gather ingredients and grill
  2. Add dogs to grill surface, flipping halfway through
  3. Add to bun after dog is crispy
  4. Start serving, adding whatever toppings you desire!
