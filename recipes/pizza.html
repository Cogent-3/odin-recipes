The 2023 pizza recipe sweeping the nation

This recipe will put aunt Kim and cousin Sheila to shame.

AND its quite simple

This dish is a fan favorite at classroom events, highschool parties, and late night snacks. Everyone loves bean pizza.

The ingredients are as follows:

  • pre made dough
  • red sauce
  • Mozerella Cheese

The steps to make it are quite easy too

  1. Gather ingredients and pan
  2. preheat oven to 400*
  3. spread out dough to have thick crust
  4. Add sauce and cheese
  5. put in oven for 20 minutes
